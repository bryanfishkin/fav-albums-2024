favorite albums of 2024

20. johnny blue skies (sturgill simpson) - passage du desir

in a year when many big artists turned to country (more on that later!), those already in the space delivered some quality stuff as well. sturgill > zach bryan, imo.

favorite tracks: "jupiter's faerie", "right kind of dream"

Listen to *passage du desir* on Spotify

19. nxworries - why lawd?

more of the classic nxworries sound, but more melancholy this time around. strong contributions from thundercat, h.e.r., and earl sweatshirt.

favorite tracks: "86sentra", "keepher", "where i go"

Listen to *why lawd?* on Spotify

18. l'impératrice - pulsar

my first experience with this french pop/alternative group. an extremely catchy album.

favorite tracks: "amour ex machina", "danza marilù", "sweet & sublime"

Listen to *pulsar* on Spotify

17. waxahatchee - tigers blood

a wonderful "outside album", it's a shame i didn't get to this 'til december.

favorite tracks: "ice cold", "right back to it", "bored", "tigers blood"

Listen to *tigers blood* on Spotify

16. jpegmafia - i lay down my life for you

track 1's opening line ("if i was an nba player, i'd be dillon brooks, but worse.") perfectly encapsulates the energy of the enitre album.

favorite tracks: "i scream this in the mirror before i interact with anyone", "sin miedo", "jpegultra!"

Listen to *i lay down my life for you* on Spotify

15. post malone - f-1 trillion

went into it expecting to roll my eyes the entire time. came out of it pleasantly surprised.

favorite tracks: "i had some help", "right about you"

Listen to *f-1 trillion* on Spotify

14. doechii - alligator bites never heal

on full display in her Tiny Desk performance, doechii's charisma and talent have her poised to blow up in 2025.

favorite tracks: "denial is a river", "nissan altima"

Listen to *alligator bites never heal* on Spotify

13. vampire weekend - only god was above us

another worthy addition to the vw discography, filled with classic and new sounds alike.

favorite tracks: "classical", "gen-x cops", "mary boone", "pravda"

Listen to *only god was above us* on Spotify

12. jamie xx - in waves

nearly a decade after "in colour", jamie xx delivers some of his danciest tracks to date.

favorite tracks: "baddy on the floor", "life", "all you children"

Listen to *in waves* on Spotify

11. grande mahogany - as grande as

like if steve lacy leaned even more into funk/rock.

favorite tracks: "super rocker", "garden séfine", "outro - the sound is through"

Listen to *as grande as* on Spotify

10. this is lorelei - box for buddy, box for star

a fun and varied indie album with effective use of auto-tune. alex g is smiling somewhere.

favorite tracks: "dancing in the club", "box for buddy, box for star"

Listen to *box for buddy, box for star* on Spotify

9. yasmin williams - acadia

an absolutely stunning largely-acoustic, largely-instrumental folk album, top to bottom.

favorite tracks: "harvest", "hummingbird"

Listen to *acadia* on Spotify

8. still woozy - loveseat

a more-than-worthy follow-up to his 2021 debut.

favorite tracks: "shotput", "all your life"

Listen to *loveseat* on Spotify

7. mj lenderman - manning fireworks

more depth than older songs like "hangover game", but still with that patented silliness (see: a lightning mcqueen reference).

favorite tracks: "rudolph", "wristwatch", "she's leaving you"

Listen to *manning fireworks* on Spotify

6. kendrick lamar - gnx

a fitting exclamation point to an insane year for mr. lamar. "tv off" will go crazy at the super bowl.

favorite tracks: "squabble up", "tv off", "heart pt. 6"

Listen to *gnx* on Spotify

5. geordie greep - the new sound

the gnarliest rock opera (?) you'll hear all year.

favorite tracks: "holy, holy", "the new sound", "if you are but a dream"

Listen to *the new sound* on Spotify

4. tyler, the creator - chromakopia

simultaneously includes some of his biggest bangers and most personal songs to date. bonus points for the young buck sample!

favorite tracks: "noid", "darling, i", "sticky", "balloon"

Listen to *chromakopia* on Spotify

3. beyoncé - cowboy carter

if you can get past the "iS iT rEaLlY a CoUnTrY aLbUm ThO?" discourse, you'll find an exceptionally fun and well-executed installment in the current beyoncé trilogy.

favorite tracks: "texas hold'em", "bodyguard", "levii's jeans", "tyrant"

Listen to *cowboy carter* on Spotify

2. charli xcx - brat

i mean you get it.

favorite tracks: "360", "von dutch", "mean girls"

Listen to *brat* on Spotify

1. porter robinson - smile! :D

a fascinating reflection on fame and its side effects, to the sound of... metro station? 3oh!3?

favorite tracks: "knock yourself out xd", "cheerleader", "perfect pinterest garden", "year of the cup"

Listen to *smile! :D* on Spotify
